# Provera Cena 📦

🇬🇧 [English](#english) | 🇷🇸 [Srpski](#srpski)

---

## English

**Provera Cena** (Price Checker) is an Android app for fast barcode scanning and price lookup directly from your own Excel or CSV price list.

### 📱 Download

Latest release: **[Releases](../../releases/latest)**

Download `BarcodeProveraCena-release.apk` and install it on your Android device.

> ⚠️ Android may ask for permission to install from unknown sources. Go to **Settings → Apps → Special Access → Install Unknown Apps** and allow installation.

---

### ✨ Features

- 📷 **Ultra-fast barcode scanning** using the camera (Google ML Kit)
- 📊 **Reads your own Excel (.xlsx, .xls) or CSV price list** – no server needed
- ☁️ **Cloud support**: Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive or any direct URL
- 📶 **Offline mode**: once downloaded, the app works without internet
- 🔦 **Torch/flashlight** for scanning in low light
- 🔍 **Text search** by product name
- 🌐 **Serbian and English** language support

---

### ⚙️ Column Mapping

In the app settings, enter the **column letter** from your Excel file:

| Field | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Product Name | `A` (1st column) |
| Price | `B` (2nd column) |
| Barcode | `G` (7th column) |

---

### 📋 Supported Data Formats

| Format | Supported |
|--------|-----------|
| Excel `.xlsx` | ✅ |
| Excel `.xls` | ✅ |
| CSV (`,` `;` `TAB` `\|`) | ✅ |

---

### 🔗 Setting Up Your Data Source

**Google Drive**
1. Open your file in Google Drive
2. Click **Share → General access → Anyone with the link**
3. Copy the link and paste it into the app

**Dropbox**
1. Click **Copy Link** next to your file
2. Paste the link into the app

---

### 📋 Requirements

- Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher
- Camera permission
- Internet connection (for cloud sources; not needed for local files)

## Srpski

**Provera Cena** je Android aplikacija za brzu proveru cena skeniranjem bar-koda, direktno iz vašeg Excel ili CSV cenovnika.

### 📱 Preuzimanje

Poslednje izdanje: **[Releases](../../releases/latest)**

Preuzmite `BarcodeProveraCena-release.apk` i instalirajte ga na vašem Android uređaju.

> ⚠️ Moguće je da će Android zatražiti dozvolu za instalaciju iz nepoznatih izvora. Idite na **Podešavanja → Aplikacije → Poseban pristup → Instaliraj nepoznate aplikacije** i dozvolite instalaciju.

---

### ✨ Funkcionalnosti

- 📷 **Ultra-brzo skeniranje** bar-kodova kamerom (Google ML Kit)
- 📊 **Direktno čita vaš Excel (.xlsx, .xls) ili CSV cenovnik** – bez servera
- ☁️ **Cloud podrška**: Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive ili direktan URL
- 📶 **Offline rad**: posle prvog preuzimanja, aplikacija radi i bez interneta
- 🔦 **Blic/lampa** za skeniranje u mraku
- 🔍 **Tekstualna pretraga** po nazivu artikla
- 🌐 **Srpski i engleski** jezik

---

### ⚙️ Podešavanje kolona

U podešavanjima aplikacije možete uneti **slovo kolone** iz vašeg Excela:

| Polje | Primer |
|-------|--------|
| Naziv artikla | `A` (prva kolona) |
| Cena | `B` (druga kolona) |
| Barcode | `G` (sedma kolona) |

---

### 📋 Podržani formati baze podataka

| Format | Podržano |
|--------|----------|
| Excel `.xlsx` | ✅ |
| Excel `.xls` | ✅ |
| CSV (`,` `;` `TAB` `\|`) | ✅ |

---

### 🔗 Podešavanje izvora podataka

**Google Drive**
1. Otvorite fajl u Google Drive-u
2. Kliknite **Deli → Opšti pristup → Bilo ko sa linkom**
3. Kopirajte link i nalepite u aplikaciju

**Dropbox**
1. Kliknite **Kopiraj link** pored fajla u Dropbox-u
2. Nalepite link u aplikaciju

---

### 📋 Sistemski zahtevi

- Android 7.0 (API 24) ili noviji
- Dozvola za kameru
- Internet konekcija (za cloud izvore; nije potrebna za lokalne fajlove)
